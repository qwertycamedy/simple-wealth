How to

Two simple steps

Step 01

We are regulated by Swiss legislation and have to do a few important things.

Since you are not our client, we need to register you, identify yourself online and follow AML procedures.

Step 02

The second part is the survey itself. We will ask 10-15 questions online about you, your life, goals and finances.

After the survey, our algorithm will calculate your risk profile and select securities to invest in. You will receive an invitation to sign the contract online, + we will send the original by mail.

Step 02

Verification

Complete the online verification. You will see a pop-up window — take a picture of the QR code and take a selfie + upload your utility bill or any document with your address confirmation. It is also a requirement by law and our financial regulator (FINMA).

Start now
Step 03

Get a preview

To understand your goals, investment expectations and investment horizon, we ask you to answer roughly 15 questions. Answers are used by our unique algorithm that calculates the risks and gives you an approximate investment outcome.

The questionnaire may feel long and too personal to you, but at the end, our team can view only the result - the calculated risk level (1-2-3-4-5). It is all without any human involvement.

You can also change this level by yourself and get more risk and potentially more return.

Try now
Step 04

Consent to the terms and conditions

At the end, you need to agree to the terms of service and the contract's digital version.

What happens then?
The data we have collected will be sent to our Swiss Custody bank to open an account.

The account will be opened under your name, and you will also receive access by separate email directly from the bankers.
